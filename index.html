From Personal Experience to Patient Experience


Alzheimer's disease has touched our lives in different ways. Our team at LANSA wanted to better the lives of patients with dementia by creating something which would help them remember. This lead to the creation of a non-profit, Suvien, where we utilize new technology to show patients their very own story.

Leveraging Web and Mobile Technology


Suvien helps patients with dementia recall memories of their lives and loved ones. Family and friends can upload the patient's photos and memories to the Suvien application. Patients use the tablet app where they can touch a photo to read captions and launch music, video or voice messages. This provides familiarity and comfort for them, by drawing out memories from their own life history.

That's how they will feel at ease and at home

OUR PARTNERS


Our project connected with The Ontario Brain Institute and the Alzheimer Society Ontario, who welcomed the project's facilitation and growth.
